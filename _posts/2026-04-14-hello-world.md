this is my first blog post. just testing things out.

i wanted a simple place to write down thoughts without the overhead of a full publishing platform. so here we are -- a bare-bones blog on my personal site.

## how this works

i write posts in markdown (or use [prose.io](https://prose.io) for a nicer editor), push to github, and the site rebuilds automatically. no databases, no logins, no nonsense.
