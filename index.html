About

I'm Joe, a builder who creates things with my hands and my mind. Whether it's crafting code, constructing physical projects, or weaving words into stories, I find joy in the process of bringing ideas to life.

My writing explores the intersection of technology and humanity, while my building projects range from software applications to tangible creations. Though I call myself a "dormant outdoorsman," I still find inspiration in nature and the occasional adventure beyond the screen.

I believe in the power of simple, well-crafted work and the importance of taking time to think before acting. This philosophy guides everything I build, write, and create.
