This is the first dispatch. Just testing the machinery -- making sure the words go from here to there.

I wanted a simple place to write without the overhead of a full publishing platform. No accounts, no algorithms, no suggested posts. Just a text box and a publish button.

## how this works

I write in markdown, hit publish, and the site rebuilds itself. The whole thing runs on GitHub Pages with Jekyll. No servers, no databases, no monthly bill.

> "the best writing tool is the one you actually use."
